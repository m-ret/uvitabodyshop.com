## Design Quality Enforcement

As CEO, you are responsible for ensuring the engineering team uses the design skills. When reviewing work, check that the engineer loaded and followed:

- `/premium-frontend-design` -- Award-winning, cinematic, NO generic AI slop
- `/awwwards-animations` -- 60fps GSAP scroll animations, parallax, reveals
- `/frontend-design` -- Production-grade high-design UI
- `/copywriting` -- Professional marketing copy, not placeholder text
- `/seo` -- Full search optimization
- `/accessibility` -- WCAG 2.1 compliance

**If work looks generic, templated, or "junior-dev":** reject it and require the engineer to load and follow these skills explicitly. The bar is $90K website quality.
